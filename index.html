Purpose

An estimated 5.3 million Americans suffer from a social phobia, with 74% suffering from speech anxiety, meaning that out of all the phobias out there, public speaking is considered one of the highest. It affects 3 out of 4 people or between 72-75% of the population fears public speaking. While there are a few that do not experience the anxiety associated with speaking, the rest of us can use some strategies to become more skilled and relaxed speakers.

That's why TeachyouSpeech aims to help individuals thrive in their public speaking endeavors by using AI to give you real-time feedback!
